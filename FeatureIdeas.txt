Normal mode:
    *Fighting:
        -turn based, you go first, then the enemy, based on your speed (so when it's your turn, you take all your actions)
        -bad status effects decrease by half per turn

Veteran mode:
    *Fighting:
        -first, you plan out your moves, then the enemies and you move at the same time (meaning the one with the quickest move speed goes firts, then next etc...)
        -enemies coordinate more
        -bad status effects decrease by 1 stack per turn
        -start with a less time, but proportionally smaller turn lenght limit decay
